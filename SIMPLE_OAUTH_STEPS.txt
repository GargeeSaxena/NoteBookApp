□ Step 1: Go to https://console.cloud.google.com
          Sign in with Google account

□ Step 2: Create/Select a project
          - Click project dropdown at top
          - Create new project: "My Notes App" OR select existing

□ Step 3: Enable Google+ API
          - Left sidebar: "APIs & Services" → "Library"
          - Search: "Google+ API"
          - Click "ENABLE"

□ Step 4: Configure OAuth Consent Screen
          - Left sidebar: "OAuth consent screen"
          - Select: "External"
          - App name: "My Notes App"
          - Your email (pre-filled)
          - Click through and save

□ Step 5: Create OAuth Client ID
          - Left sidebar: "Credentials"
          - Click: "+ CREATE CREDENTIALS"
          - Select: "OAuth client ID"
          - Type: "Web application"
          - Name: "My Notes Web App"
          
          Add Authorized redirect URIs (IMPORTANT!):
          👉 https://mkxytkpewzmaucgwhahs.supabase.co/auth/v1/callback
          
          - Click "CREATE"

□ Step 1: Go to https://app.supabase.com
          Sign in to your account

□ Step 2: Select project "note-app-c70ad"

□ Step 3: Go to Authentication
          - Left sidebar: "Authentication" (shield icon)
